Our Story

Located in the heart of Palm Beach at Paseo Herencia Mall, Che Bar brings island soul to Aruba. Start your day with our beloved Dutch pancakes, fresh bagels, empanadas, breakfast burritos, and made-to-order eggs. Wind down at our daily happy hour from 5–7 PM, sip handcrafted cocktails late into the night, and catch live music multiple times a week. From sunrise breakfasts to fresh, hot, made-to-order wood-fired pizzas under the stars — every moment at Che Bar is a celebration.
